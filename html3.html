Life without words is kinda boring. Luckily, in HTML, that's not the case. In HTML, to express words, we can use h1, h2, h3, h4, h5, h6, and p. The h in h1, h2, h3, h4, h5, and h6 is just an abstract way to measure size. h1 is the largest and h6 is the smallest for heading as seen below. p stands for paragraph. You can change the font-size, which we will see in out CSS tutorial. Because this something that is rendered, you would put this all in your body.
